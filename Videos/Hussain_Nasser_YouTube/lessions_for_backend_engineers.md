# Lessons for backend engineers

1. Take your time in learning new stuff.
2. Stay Hungry and keep learning. The container of knowledge will never be filled. There is always new stuff to learn.
3. Listen to your colleagues. Assume that every one of your colleagues knows something you don't know.
4. Never ever bad mouth your collegue's code
5. Question everything
