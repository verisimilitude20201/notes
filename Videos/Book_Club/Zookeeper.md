# Relational Vs Document Models
1. How the databases are layered. Real world objects get converted into OO-classes and objects. This gets converted into JSON objects, tables. This gets converted into memories, bytes. These are then represented by pulses of light, electric signals in hardware.

2. Relational databases: Most popular. Used for banking transactions, customer invoicing, payroll. Also for batch-mode systems.
